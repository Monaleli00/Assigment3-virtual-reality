THABA BOSIU
Mountain at Night
Thaba Bosiu is an important mountain in Lesotho and is known as the birthplace of the Basotho nation. King Moshoeshoe I chose this mountain around 1824 because it was high and easy to defend, giving his people safety. Today, visitors come to Thaba Bosiu because of its strong history and natural beauty. Many people climb the mountain to experience the journey, explore the rocky paths, and see wide views of Lesotho from the top. It is not just a mountain — it is a place where history, culture, and adventure meet.
